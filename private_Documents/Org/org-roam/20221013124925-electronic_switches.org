#+title: Electronic Switches

Some Electronic Switches include; [[id:5cbc3c62-57b7-43b6-9036-12c2fc97a394][Electronic Valves]] (Vacuum Tubes) or [[id:5fbbea7b-9161-47b2-ba21-1329d6077d0f][Transistors]]
