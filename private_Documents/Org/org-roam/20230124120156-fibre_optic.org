#+title: Fibre Optic
Fibre Optics are most commonly used in [[id:81d717e0-7bbe-4d42-a481-d801667e3a85][Network]]s to connect hardware together to facilitate communication.
They carry light through the strand located at the centre of the ’wire’, the light is usually laserlight.
The data is flashes of light that represent [[id:69c50131-9f37-4a62-b146-f75b13117f3b][Binary Number]]s.
