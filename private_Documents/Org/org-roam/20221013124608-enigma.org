#+title: Enigma
#+filetags: :draft:
Enigma was a cipher machine used mainly by the German military to protect information. It was cracked with the use of a specialised electronic [[id:31558a7a-be7b-4672-899c-2c7abfe704fd][Computer]] called [[id:f6e07465-a1c5-403d-8986-bb605f16e271][Colossus]].
